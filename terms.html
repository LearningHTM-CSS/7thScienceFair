A Caffeine Kick

Terms:

Daphnia Magna: Microbial aquatic organism

Caffeine: A bitter alkaloid C8H10N4O2 found especially in coffee, tea, cacao, and kola nuts and used medicinally as a stimulant and diuretic

Epinephrine:A crystalline sympathomimetic hormone C9H13NO3 that is the principal blood-pressure raising hormone secreted by the medulla of the adrenal glands; also known as adrenaline
